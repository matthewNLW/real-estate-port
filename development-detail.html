The Meridian at Southline

Charlotte, NC

A 4.2-acre mixed-use development in Charlotte's emerging Southline district. The project brings together thoughtful residential design with ground-floor retail and dining, creating a walkable neighborhood anchored by green space and connectivity.

Project Vision

The Meridian represents our commitment to creating places that enhance daily life. Located at the intersection of Charlotte's established South End and the emerging Southline district, the development capitalizes on exceptional transit access while preserving the neighborhood's scale and character.

Ground-floor retail spaces are designed for local businesses—cafés, fitness studios, and neighborhood services—rather than formula retail. The residential units range from studios to three-bedrooms, with floor plans that prioritize light, storage, and thoughtful layouts over superficial finishes.

Location

Southline is Charlotte's newest transit-oriented district, connected to Uptown via the LYNX Blue Line. The Meridian sits within a five-minute walk of the New Bern station, providing residents with car-optional living and direct access to employment centers, entertainment, and dining throughout the region.
